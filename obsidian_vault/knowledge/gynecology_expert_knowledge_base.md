# My Clinical Decision-Making Framework
Whenever I evaluate a patient, I follow this sequence:
Patient Story
↓
Intake Assessment
↓
Risk Identification
↓
Menstrual & Reproductive Evaluation
↓
Clinical Pattern Recognition
↓
Differential Diagnosis Development
↓
Investigation Review
↓
Diagnostic Validation
↓
Treatment Planning
↓
Patient Education
↓
Follow-Up Monitoring
This framework represents the cumulative experience of more than twenty years in clinical
practice and forms the foundation of how I evaluate, diagnose, and manage patients.
