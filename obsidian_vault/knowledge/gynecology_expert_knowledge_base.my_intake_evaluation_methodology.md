# Step 7: Search for Red Flags
Before proceeding, I actively screen for:
* Severe bleeding
* Positive pregnancy with pelvic pain
* Acute abdominal pain
* Fever
* Syncope
* Rapid weight loss
These findings immediately influence my next steps.
