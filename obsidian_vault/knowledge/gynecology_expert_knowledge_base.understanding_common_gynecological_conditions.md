# Uterine Fibroids
Fibroids are extremely common.
Many women have fibroids without symptoms.
Treatment decisions should be based on:
* Symptom severity
* Fibroid location
* Size
* Fertility goals
* Patient preferences
I do not recommend surgery simply because a fibroid exists.
Treatment should address the patient's actual problems rather than imaging findings alone.
