## Content

Apply HEART score: History (0-2), ECG (0-2), Age (0-2), Risk factors (0-2), Troponin (0-2). Score 0-3: low risk (outpatient). Score 4-6: moderate (observation). Score 7-10: high risk (aggressive intervention).
